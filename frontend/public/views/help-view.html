CampusHelper Guide

Search & Find

Use the search bar at the top or browse the directory to find buildings. Every building has a specific icon for identification. You can search by building name or category (like "Academic" or "Services").

Precision Routing

Select a building and tap "Get Directions". We'll draw a precise walking path from your GPS position directly to the entrance. Ensure your GPS is enabled for the best experience.

Smart Sharing

Send locations to friends using the share button. They'll receive a link that opens the map exactly on that spot. Perfect for meeting up!

Quick Controls

Use the "My Location" button on the map to instantly center on your current position. The map works best in high-accuracy GPS mode on mobile devices.

Privacy & Data

Your location data is processed locally on your device and never stored on our servers. We use open-source mapping technology to ensure your privacy while navigating.

Support & Feedback

Spotted an incorrect building name or coordinate? Let us know through your department head or visit the IT office in the Information Technology Department.
